Introduction

TechnoBuffalo's CMS is powered by Wordpress. The Wordpress theme was developed in part by Parapxl, and was original launched around the end of 2011 / beginning of 2012. The site that is currently live on TechnoBuffalo.com is the third version, otherwise known as "TechnoBuffalo 3" or "TechnoBuffalo 3.0".

Technology

Wordpress / PHP

The Wordpress theme is a child theme of Parapxl's pxl framework. Since it's original launch, much of the theme's setup/function methods have been heavily modified to suite advertising and feature needs.

Below is the breakdown/location of the core .php based files of the theme: 

technobuffalo/
├── templates/
│   ├── custom
│   ├── default
│   ├── js
│   ├── partials
├── setup.php

setup.php
